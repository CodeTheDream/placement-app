About

Purpose

The Home Page is a browser-based web application for CRH social workers to find and exchange information about licensed facilities in North Carolina. The purpose of the project is to improve the quality of information available to social workers, so we can spend less time searching for options and more time working with our patients. This will hopefully result in patients spending less time waiting for placement to be found, and being matched with placements that are a good fit.

FAQ

How will this help me find placements?

This is a tool to help guide the search process, but doesn't replace your clinical and case management expertise in
assisting a person with finding housing or placement.

Use the text search and/or filters to build a list of placements that seem suitable for your patient. Add these placements to your cart and download it into a pre-formatted Excel spreadsheet to use as a note-taking template for making calls. Check the announcements page to see if an opening has been posted recently, and browse the comments to find out additional details that may be relevant to your search. When you're done, come back and post what you've learned to help someone else out!

What’s in the database?

As of the most recent update, 5600A group home placements statewide are available in the database. The information is obtained from the DHSR licensed facilities listing. Some details have also been pulled from existing CRH “Recommended Placements” documents. Family Care Homes and Adult Care Homes are in the process of being added, and updates are consistently made based on information contributed by social workers.

The administrator will also update existing information based on comments and announcements made by users like you. When you learn a new piece of information about a placement or licensee, please notify the administrator via one of the methods described below.

How do I add my updates to the site?

You can add comments about individual placements by navigating to the page for that placement and scrolling down to the comments section. Especially helpful would be the gender served at the facility, and the contact person for referrals along with their phone/fax numbers (especially if this is different than what is listed on the page). Consider other info like special funding needed, specific age range or diagnosis, HUD or other application types, or special populations that will/will not be considered (sex offenders, legal background).

You can also make announcements about bed openings or other time-sensitive matters on the Announcements page.

If you’ve used a downloaded spreadsheet to take notes and have logged bits of info about a lot of placements which would be tedious to go back and add individual comments about, feel free to email your spreadsheet to Lauren Sheridan and she will happily add your information to the database during the next round of updates.

Thank you in advance for giving back to your fellow SWs by adding updated info to the site!

Is this site secure?

The website is secured using a variety of methods including HTTPS and access limitations built into the source code. Users cannot self-register, they can only be added by an administrator. The site is only accessible by CRH social workers who have requested credentials.

The site is not HIPAA-compliant and therefore should not contain any PHI. The administrator reviews and removes any PHI as well as content that may be considered unprofessional or disrespectful.
