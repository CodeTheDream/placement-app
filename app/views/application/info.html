About

Purpose

The Home Page is a browser-based web application for social workers and other supporters of people with mental illness to find and exchange information about licensed residential placements in North Carolina. The purpose of the project is to improve the quality of information available to providers, so we can spend less time on the logistics of searching for options and more time working directly with people. This should subsequently result in prospective residents spending less time waiting for placement to be found, and having a better chance of being matched with a placement that is a good fit for them.

FAQ

How will this help me find placements?

This is a tool to help guide the search process, but doesn't replace your clinical and case management expertise in assisting an individual with finding housing or placement.

Use the text search and/or filters to build a list of placements that seem suitable for your client or patient. Add these placements to your cart and download it into a pre-formatted Excel spreadsheet to use as a note-taking template for making calls. Check the announcements page to see if an opening has been posted recently, and browse the comments on each placement's page to find out additional details that may be relevant to your search. As you search, please post what you've learned to help someone else out!

What’s in the database?

All DHSR-licensed 5600A group homes and family care homes statewide are available in the database. The information is obtained from the DHSR licensed facilities listing which is updated roughly every month. New information is continuously added by users via the Announcements page and comments on individual placement pages within this site, and this is reviewed and added to the database by an administrator where appropriate (for example, changes to referral contact info).

The administrator will also update existing information based on comments and announcements made by users like you. When you learn a new piece of information about a placement or licensee, please notify the administrator via one of the methods described below.

How do I add my updates to the site?

You can add comments about individual placements by navigating to the page for that placement and scrolling down to the comments section. Especially helpful would be the gender served at the facility, and the contact person for referrals along with their phone/fax numbers (especially if this is different than what is listed on the page). Consider other info like special funding needed, specific age range or diagnosis, HUD or other application types, or special populations that will/will not be considered (sex offenders, legal background).

You can also make announcements about bed openings or other time-sensitive matters on the Announcements page.

If you’ve used a downloaded cart spreadsheet to take notes and have logged bits of info about a lot of placements which would be tedious to go back and add individual comments about, please feel free to email your spreadsheet to admin@homepagecarolina.org so that it can be reviewed and incorporated into the next update. Please do not send PHI to this address!

Thank you in advance for giving back to your fellow providers by contributing information!

Is this site secure?

The website is secured using a variety of methods including HTTPS and access limitations built into the source code. Users cannot self-register, they can only be added by an administrator. The site is only accessible by social workers or other approved providers who have requested credentials.

The site is not HIPAA-compliant and therefore should not contain any PHI. The administrator reviews and removes any PHI as well as content that may be considered unprofessional or disrespectful.

What if I need more help?

Pleasse feel welcome to send questions and feedback to Lauren Sheridan, the site's owner and administrator. You can also direct your colleagues to contact this address for access requests. Again, please do not send any information specific to your clients or patients via this website or email address.
